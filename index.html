Kanji Manuscript Annotation Project

This is a small experimental site exploring how kanji in historical Japanese texts can be annotated for meaning and reading.

Sample Kanji

道 — michi (road, way)

Seen in travel texts like the Tōkaidō route. Appears in Edo-period manuscripts.

Image Example

  • Kanji: 月
  • Reading: つき (*tsuki*)
  • Meaning: Moon
  • Context: Appears in the title 廓の月 (“Moon of the Pleasure Quarters”)
  • Artist: Tsukioka Yoshitoshi (1839–1892)
  • Series: One Hundred Aspects of the Moon (1885–1892)
